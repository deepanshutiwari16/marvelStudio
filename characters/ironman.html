Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a genius billionaire, inventor, and philanthropist in the Marvel Universe. Created by Stan Lee, he first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. After suffering a life-threatening chest injury, Stark built a high-tech armored suit to save his life and escape captivity. Using his intelligence and vast resources, he continuously upgrades his suit with advanced weapons, flight capabilities, and artificial intelligence. As a founding member of the Avengers, Iron Man plays a crucial role in defending the world from powerful threats.
