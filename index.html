Avengers

The Avengers are a team of Earth's mightiest heroes in the Marvel Universe, assembled to protect the world from powerful threats. Founded by Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Captain America, Black Widow, and Hawkeye, the team battles villains like Loki, Ultron, and Thanos. Known for their unity and strength, they fight to defend humanity, embodying the motto "Avengers, Assemble!" Over time, the team has expanded, including heroes like Black Panther, Doctor Strange, and Spider-Man, making them one of Marvel’s most iconic superhero teams.
