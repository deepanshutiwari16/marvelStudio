Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is a Marvel superhero created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. He gained his powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider, which gave him enhanced strength, agility, and the ability to cling to walls.

Genius. Billionaire. Philanthropist. Tony Stark's confidence is only matched by his high-flying abilities as the hero called Iron Man.
